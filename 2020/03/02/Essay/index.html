首先声明一下我对好文章的评判标准。我认为的好文章，应该是主题明确，逻辑清晰的，其次才是文章的文采，所以文章中主要讨论的是文章的结构内容。
先分析下顾老师的写的内容：

Real-time multimedia data delivery over the Internet is complex. There are many challenging problems in improving the performances of the real-time data delivery.

A platform which can simulate the whole process of real-time video sharing will provide an opportunity to analyze the real-time multimedia data delivery applications, and thus provide a tool to improve the performances of the data delivery.

This essay describes a project on designing and implementing a platform for simulating the whole process of real-time video sharing.

语言简练，但句子之间逻辑清晰。 本段的核心内容是设计一款程序，这里可以有几个层面扩展：原因，作用，提升表现，概括文章内容。

introduction中，需要介绍几个要素：这个项目的目的是什么，讲述现状，如何实现项目。

Real-time Video Share

A real-time video sharing plateform simulates a video telephony environment, which provides clients with online services. These services show as following:

  1. Review video and audio share. Support clients to review video pictures and voices from computer camera and microphone.
  2. Delay control. Decrease time delay to meet the interactive request.
  3. Data security. Encrypt transmission data during a video share from server to client.

The media sources exchanged in a real-time video sharing platform is one kind of CM sources.
